About Geeks Gala 3.0

Geeks Gala 3.0 is the annual technical fest organized by the Computer Science Department of K.V. Pendharkar College. It is a celebration of technology, creativity, logic, and fun.

The event brings together students from various colleges to compete, collaborate, and showcase their skills across a wide range of technical and gaming events. From mind-bending quizzes and hackathons to intense esports battles, Geeks Gala offers something for every tech enthusiast.

With exciting challenges, innovative formats, and a vibrant competitive atmosphere, Geeks Gala 3.0 aims to inspire learning, teamwork, and innovation beyond classrooms.
