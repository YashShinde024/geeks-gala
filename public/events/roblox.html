Clutch The Chain – Roblox Event

Trust the Chain.

Clutch The Chain is an action-packed Roblox gaming event where teams compete to climb as high as possible in the game Chain Together. The event tests coordination, timing, and clutch decision-making under pressure.

The competition follows an Elimination + Final Challenge format with predefined checkpoints and a clear scoring system.

Team Formation

  • Teams of minimum 2 and maximum 3 participants
  • Only the first 20 registered teams will be considered

Event Format & Plan of Action

  • Phase 1 – Qualifier Round: Each team is allowed only 5 attempts to climb. An attempt ends if the team falls or the chain breaks. Points are awarded based on the highest checkpoint reached in any one attempt. Checkpoint scores are not cumulative. After 5 attempts, the team is eliminated from qualifiers. The Top 10 teams qualify for the Final Round.
  • Phase 2 – Final Round (Endgame): The Top 10 teams are allowed continuous attempts. The first team to reach the top wins instantly. If no team reaches the top, rankings are decided by the highest checkpoint achieved.
  • In case of a tie, a tie-breaker round will be conducted if necessary.

Checkpoint & Scoring System

  • Checkpoints will be clearly marked and announced before the event.
  • Points are awarded only for the highest checkpoint reached.
  • Screenshot or volunteer verification is mandatory.

Winner Selection

  • Instant Win: First team to reach the top.
  • If no instant win, rankings are decided based on checkpoint scores.

Rules & Regulations

  • Each team is allowed only 5 attempts in the Qualifier Round.
  • Checkpoint scores are not cumulative.
  • Screenshot or volunteer verification is mandatory.
  • Laptops as well as mobile phones are allowed.
  • Teams who wish to use lab PCs must report early, as systems will be provided on a First Come, First Served (FCFS) basis.
  • Participants must create their Roblox account on the spot, 30 minutes before the event.
  • Only the first 20 registered teams will be eligible to participate.
  • Any form of unfair play, cheating, or manipulation will result in immediate disqualification.
  • The decisions of the judges and organizing committee are final and binding.
