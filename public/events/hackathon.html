Shadow Commit

Build. Break. Adapt.

Shadow Commit is an intense on-site hackathon organized by the Computer Science Students of K.V. Pendharkar College under Geeks Gala 3.0.

Teams will work against the clock to design, build, and present a solution to a common problem statement — with a twist introduced during the event that will test adaptability, teamwork, and decision-making.

  • Total Duration: 3 Hours
  • Team Size: Exactly 3 participants
  • Mode: On-site (Computer Lab / Personal Laptops allowed)

Plan of Action

  • Participants report to the lab and occupy their assigned systems.
  • Organizers explain the problem statement, time limit, and general rules.
  • Teams begin coding once the hackathon starts.
  • A special in-event activity is introduced during the hackathon.
  • Teams continue development as per the instructions provided at that time.
  • At the end of 3 hours, teams submit their project for evaluation.
  • Final results are announced after judging.

Rules & Regulations

  • Each team must consist of exactly 3 members.
  • Teams may use assigned systems or bring their own laptops.
  • The hackathon duration is strictly limited to 3 hours.
  • All teams will receive the same problem statement.
  • Participants must strictly follow instructions given by the organizers.
  • Any role or information assigned during the event must remain confidential.
  • Discussion or disclosure of assigned roles will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Misconduct, arguments, or disruption will result in disqualification.
  • Projects must be submitted before the deadline with a short explanation.
  • Judging will be based on:
    • UI & basic functionality
    • Presentation
    • A special judging element revealed during evaluation
  • Judges’ and organizing committee decisions are final and binding.
