Booyah Bash – Free Fire Event

Get ready to drop, loot, and dominate!

Booyah Bash is an Inter-College Garena Free Fire tournament organized by the Department Of Computer Science of K.V. Pendharkar College under Geeks Gala 3.0.

Show your skills, teamwork, and strategy as you compete against top teams from various colleges and stand a chance to win exciting cash prizes.

  • Game: Garena Free Fire
  • Entry Fee: ₹100 per team
  • Team Format: Squad of 4 players
  • Total Slots: Limited to 36 teams

Rules & Regulations

  • College ID Mandatory: All players must carry a valid College ID.
  • Mobile Only: Emulators, tablets/iPads, controllers, triggers, or external tools are strictly prohibited.
  • Game Readiness: All maps and resources must be downloaded before the event.
  • Punctuality: Teams must join the lobby on time.
  • Player Conduct: Abusive language or arguments with organizers are not allowed.
  • Fair Play: Hacks, mods, scripts, GFX tools, or teaming will lead to immediate disqualification.

Disclaimer: Judges’ decisions are final. Organizers reserve the right to modify rules or schedules. Non-compliance may result in disqualification without refund.

Tournament Format

  • Phase 1 – Qualifiers: 36 teams divided into 3 groups (A, B, C), 12 teams per group. Each group plays 2 matches (Bermuda + Rotated Map). Top 4 teams from each group qualify based on cumulative score.
  • Phase 2 – Grand Final: 12 qualified teams battle in a single lobby on the Kalahari map. Top 3 teams based on final score are declared winners.
