The 90th Minute – FIFA Event

Play. Compete. Dominate.

Event Format

  • Day 1 – Round 1: 80 teams (2 players per team) compete in direct knockout matches. Teams are divided into two batches of 40 teams each. Top 20 teams from each batch qualify.
  • Day 1 – Round 2: 40 qualified teams compete in knockout matches. Top 20 teams advance to Day 2.
  • Day 2 – Round 3: 20 teams play 10 matches. 10 teams qualify for the next round.
  • Round 4: 10 teams play 5 matches. 5 teams qualify. The team with the highest number of goals receives a Bye.
  • Round 5: 4 teams play 2 matches. One team qualifies directly for the finals based on goal advantage.
  • Round 6: The Bye team plays the remaining qualifier. Winner advances to the finals. Loser moves to Sudden Death.
  • Final & Sudden Death: The final match decides the Champion. Penalty shootouts determine 2nd and 3rd positions if required.

Rules & Regulations

  • The tournament will be played on PlayStation 5 using the official FIFA / EA FC game.
  • Each team must consist of exactly 2 players. Solo or mixed teams are not allowed.
  • Maximum of 80 teams will be allowed.
  • Entry Fee: ₹200 per team (non-refundable).
  • Tournament format is knockout-based. Match schedule will be decided by the organizers.
  • Teams must report 15 minutes before their scheduled match time.
  • Match settings will be fixed and standardized for all teams.
  • PlayStation consoles and controllers will be provided. Personal controllers are not allowed.
  • Any damage to equipment will result in disqualification and penalty charges.
  • Fair play is mandatory. Abuse, cheating, or misconduct will result in immediate disqualification.
  • In case of a draw, the match will be decided by penalty shootouts.
  • Technical issues will be resolved by the organizers. Their decision will be final and binding.
  • Prizes will be awarded only to teams following all rules.
  • Organizers reserve the right to modify rules or schedules if required.
