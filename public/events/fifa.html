The 90th Minute - Fifa Event

Play. Compete. Dominate.

Event Format

  • Day 1 – Round 1: 80 teams (2 players per team) compete in direct elimination matches. Teams are divided into two batches of 40 teams each. Top 20 teams from each batch qualify for the next round.
  • Day 1 – Round 2: 40 qualified teams compete in elimination matches. Top 20 teams advance to Day 2.
  • Day 2 – Round 3: 20 teams play 10 matches. 10 teams qualify for the next round.
  • Round 4: 10 teams play 5 matches. 5 teams qualify. The team with the highest number of goals receives a Bye.
  • Round 5: 4 teams play 2 matches. One team qualifies directly for the finals based on goal advantage.
  • Round 6: The Bye team plays the remaining qualifier. Winner advances to the finals. Loser moves to Sudden Death.
  • Final & Sudden Death: The final match decides the Champion. Penalty shootouts determine 2nd and 3rd positions if required.

Rules & Regulations

  • Each team must consist of exactly 2 players.
  • Maximum of 80 teams will be allowed.
  • Entry fee is ₹100 per team.
  • All matches will be played on the provided PlayStation setups.
  • Teams must report on time for their scheduled matches.
  • Any form of unfair play, misconduct, or rule violation will lead to disqualification.
  • Judges’ and organizers’ decisions are final and binding.
