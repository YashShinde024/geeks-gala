Hunt-O-Mania - Tressure Hunt

Think fast. Hunt smart.

Hunt O Mania is an immersive logic-based puzzle hunt where participants crack clues, trace digital trails, and race against time. Organized by the Computer Science Students of K.V. Pendharkar College under Geeks Gala 3.0, this event tests your observation, problem-solving, and speed.

Rules & Regulations

  • Participants may compete individually or in teams, as specified by the organizers.
  • Only the system assigned by the organizers must be used.
  • All required files are preloaded; internet access is strictly prohibited.
  • Participants must follow the sequence of pages and must not open files directly.
  • Refreshing pages, modifying files, or attempting to bypass steps is not allowed.
  • External help, copying, or communication with others is strictly prohibited.
  • All clues must be solved independently using logic and observation.
  • Any rule violation will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Judges’ and organizers’ decisions are final and binding.

Event Format

  • Page 1 – Bank Breach Alert: Participants begin the mission with a dramatic introduction. A simple cipher or password puzzle unlocks the next page.
  • Page 2 – Suspicious Transactions: A fake bank statement is shown. Participants identify a fraudulent account or suspicious pattern.
  • Page 3 – Track the Money: A money-flow diagram is displayed. Participants trace the correct transaction path to proceed.
  • Page 4 – Final Vault: Previous answers are combined to form a final code. Successful entry displays “Vault Secured – Mission Complete”.

Completion & Scoring

  • Participants must note their total completion time.
  • A screenshot of the final success screen must be taken.
  • Winners are decided based on:
    • Fastest completion time
    • Tie-breaker: fewer wrong attempts
